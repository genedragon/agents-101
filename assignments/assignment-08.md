# Assignment 8: Make Your Agent Remember You (Advanced)

**Due:** End of session 8 (finish at home if needed)
**Estimated time:** 20–30 minutes
**Format:** KiroClaw (or OpenClaw). Notebook for reflections.

> Advanced track — give it a real try. If setup is rough, that's expected; get as far as you can and bring your blockers.

---

## Goal

Prove your agent can remember something about you across a full restart.

---

## Part 1 — Teach it three things (10 min)

Tell your agent three things to remember. For example:

> "Remember that: (1) I'm building [your project], (2) I like short plain explanations, (3) my favorite subject is [x]."

Confirm it saved each one.

---

## Part 2 — Restart and test (10 min)

**Fully restart** the agent (new session, not just a new message). Then ask:

> "What am I building, how do I like things explained, and what's my favorite subject?"

Write down: did it remember all three? Any it missed?

---

## Part 3 — Read the memory (5 min)

Find and open the memory file/notes the agent keeps. See that it's **just notes it wrote** — not magic. Write one sentence about what surprised you.

---

## Part 4 — What should it remember? (5 min)

Make two short lists in your notebook:
- **Glad it remembers:** (e.g., my projects, my preferences)
- **Should NOT store:** (e.g., passwords, other people's private info)

---

## Part 5 — Stretch (optional)

Teach it a *preference* ("always give me examples") and, in a new session, ask it something unrelated. Did it follow the preference without being reminded? Note what happened.

---

## How this gets graded

"Did you try?" We're looking for:
- Evidence it remembered across a restart
- You looked at the actual memory file
- A thoughtful "glad it remembers / should not store" list
