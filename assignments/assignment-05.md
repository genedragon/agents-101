# Assignment 5: My Project Plan + First Version

**Due:** End of session 5 (finish at home if needed)
**Estimated time:** 25–35 minutes
**Format:** Kiro IDE. Create a `PLAN.md` in your project folder.

---

## Goal

Turn your idea into a small, clear plan and get a first version that **runs** — even if it's rough.

---

## Part 1 — Let the agent interview you (10 min)

Tell the agent:

> "I want to build [your idea]. Ask me 5 questions to help me figure out exactly what it should do."

Answer its questions. This is design, not building yet.

---

## Part 2 — Write your plan (10 min)

Create `PLAN.md` and fill in these three lines:

```
WHAT IT DOES:  (one sentence)
WHO IT'S FOR:  (a real person — even if that's you)
DONE ENOUGH:   (the smallest version you'd be proud to show a friend)
```

Keep "done enough" **small**. You can always add more in weeks 6–7.

---

## Part 3 — Scaffold a first version (15 min)

Ask the agent:

> "Make a first version of this that runs, even if it's rough and ugly."

Get it to **run**. Ugly is fine. Broken-but-close is fine to keep working on. The only goal: it does *something* when you open it.

Write one line in `PLAN.md`: *does it run yet? what's the next thing to add?*

---

## Part 4 — Stretch (optional)

List 3 features you'd add if you had unlimited time, then **circle the one** that would make the biggest difference for the smallest effort. That's your week-6 target.

---

## How this gets graded

"Did you try?" We're looking for:
- A `PLAN.md` with all three lines filled in
- Your "done enough" is genuinely small (not "a whole app")
- A first version that runs, however rough

---

## Bring next week — important!

- Your project that **runs**
- A GitHub account (or be ready to make one) — check the account path with your instructor
- Any errors you couldn't fix (we'll tackle them)

---

## If you're stuck

Can't think of an idea? Pick from this menu and make it yours: a quiz, a habit tracker, a random picker, a fan page for something you love, a tiny game, a "which should I choose" tool. If your idea feels too big, ask the agent: *"what's the smallest version of this I could build first?"*
